# Vision and Scope

## Platform Purpose

Reach Developments Station is a **Real Estate Development Operating System**. It is designed to give real estate developers a single, integrated platform to manage every stage of the development lifecycle — from the first land underwriting decision through to unit delivery and financial close.

The system is purpose-built for the operational complexity of professional real estate development businesses. It is not a CRM, not a project management tool, and not a spreadsheet replacement. It is a domain-specific operating system built around how real estate developers actually think, plan, and execute.

---

## What This Platform Is

- A development lifecycle management system covering land, feasibility, pricing, sales, payment plans, collections, finance, and post-sale
- A rule-based engine for enforcing pricing logic, sales exceptions, payment plan structures, and collection governance
- A financial summary and reporting backbone that gives developers real-time visibility across their portfolio
- A platform that starts with a modular monolith and is structured for incremental capability expansion

---

## What This Platform Is Not

- Not a general-purpose CRM
- Not a construction project management tool (it tracks delivery governance, not construction scheduling)
- Not a financial accounting system (it provides development finance summaries, not a full ledger)
- Not a market listing platform
- Not a property management system (post-sale operational property management is out of scope)

---

## Who It Serves

The platform is built for:

| User Type | Primary Role |
|---|---|
| Development Director / CEO | Portfolio-level visibility, feasibility sign-off, strategic decision-making |
| Project Manager | Phase and building progress, delivery governance tracking |
| Feasibility Analyst | Scenario modelling, proforma analysis, IRR and break-even calculation |
| Pricing Manager | Unit pricing, premium rules, escalation management, override governance |
| Sales Manager | Sales pipeline, reservation and contract management, exception approvals |
| Finance Manager | Payment plan governance, collections oversight, revenue recognition reporting |
| Collections Officer | Receivables management, aging tracking, alert resolution |
| Registration Officer | Conveyancing workflow, document checklist management |

---

## Development Lifecycle Stages Covered

The platform covers the full development lifecycle as follows:

| Stage | System Coverage |
|---|---|
| **Land** | Land underwriting, valuation, residual land value calculation |
| **Concept Planning** | Scenario planning, unit mix modelling, density analysis |
| **Feasibility** | Proforma modelling, IRR, NPV, break-even, scenario comparison |
| **Cost Planning & Tender** | Cost estimation, tender comparison, variance tracking |
| **Design & Delivery Governance** | Stage gate approvals, permit tracking, consultant coordination |
| **Unit Registry** | Asset master data — project, phase, building, floor, unit hierarchy |
| **Pricing** | Base pricing, premium rules, price lists, override governance |
| **Price Escalation** | Scheduled and triggered price escalation management |
| **Sales & Contracts** | Reservation, SPA, contract management, pipeline reporting |
| **Sales Exceptions & Incentives** | Discount governance, incentive approval workflows |
| **Payment Plans** | Configurable plan templates, schedule generation, cashflow impact |
| **Collections & Receivables** | Receipt matching, aging analysis, collection alerts |
| **Revenue Recognition** | Stage-based and milestone-based recognition reporting |
| **Registration & Conveyancing** | Title transfer workflow, document checklist governance |
| **Commissions** | Agent and broker commission tracking and payable management |
| **Analytics & Market Intelligence** | Sales velocity, absorption rates, price band analysis, market benchmarks |

---

## System Boundaries

**In scope:**
- All stages listed above
- Rule-based business logic engines
- Financial summary and reporting
- Document intelligence (future phase)

**Out of scope:**
- General ledger accounting
- Property management after delivery
- Construction scheduling / ERP
- Mortgage processing
- Public-facing sales portals
- Human resources and payroll
